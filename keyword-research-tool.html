About the Keyword Research Tool

The Keyword Research Tool by Mass Tools helps you find keywords to improve your website's SEO. Enter keywords to see search volume, difficulty, and related suggestions. Important: This tool uses sample data for demonstration. For real-world SEO, consider professional tools like Ahrefs or SEMrush.

Why Use the Keyword Research Tool?

This tool simplifies keyword discovery:

  • SEO Planning: Identify high-impact keywords for your content.
  • Competitor Analysis: Find keywords your competitors might target.
  • Content Creation: Discover related keywords for blog posts or articles.
  • Marketing: Optimize PPC campaigns with keyword insights.

How to Use the Keyword Research Tool

Follow these steps:

  • Enter Keywords: Type one or more keywords, separated by commas.
  • Set Filters: Choose search volume or difficulty filters to narrow results.
  • Search: Click "Search Keywords" to view results in a table.
  • Analyze: Review search volume, difficulty, and related keywords.
  • Copy: Click "Copy Results" to copy the table to your clipboard.
  • Download: Click "Download (CSV)" to save results as a file.
  • Reset: Click "Reset" to clear inputs and results.

The tool works locally in your browser using sample data.
