About the URL Extractor

The URL Extractor by Mass Tools is a free tool to extract URLs from text, websites, or files. Ideal for web scraping, SEO, or link management, it processes data locally in your browser for privacy. Important: Ensure compliance with website Terms of Service and privacy laws when extracting URLs.

Why Use the URL Extractor?

This tool simplifies collecting URLs for various purposes:

  • Web Scraping: Extract links from pages for data analysis.
  • SEO Analysis: Gather URLs for auditing backlinks or site structure.
  • Link Management: Organize links from documents or websites.
  • Research: Collect URLs for content aggregation or crawling (with permission).

How to Use the URL Extractor

Follow these steps to extract URLs:

  • Paste Text: Copy and paste text containing URLs into the text area.
  • Enter Website URL: Provide a website URL to scan for links (ensure permission).
  • Upload File: Upload a TXT, PDF, or CSV file to extract URLs.
  • Set Options: Enable "Unique URLs" to remove duplicates, "Sort Alphabetically" for ordered output, or add a filter (e.g., example.com or https).
  • Extract: Click "Extract" to find URLs.
  • Copy: Click "Copy URLs" to copy to your clipboard.
  • Download: Click "Download (CSV)" to save as a CSV file.
  • Reset: Click "Reset" to clear inputs and settings.

The tool works locally in your browser and supports offline use after loading.
