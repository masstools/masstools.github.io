About the Online Notepad

The Online Notepad by Mass Tools is a free tool for writing, editing, and saving notes directly in your browser. With auto-save, text formatting, and file import/export, it’s perfect for note-taking, brainstorming, or text editing. Important: Avoid storing sensitive information, as notes are saved in your browser’s local storage.

Why Use the Online Notepad?

This tool simplifies note-taking and text editing:

  • Note-Taking: Jot down ideas, to-do lists, or meeting notes.
  • Text Editing: Edit text with basic formatting (bold, italic, underline).
  • Productivity: Use auto-save to retain notes across sessions.
  • Data Import/Export: Import text from files or export notes as TXT/CSV.

How to Use the Online Notepad

Follow these steps to use the notepad:

  • Write Notes: Type or paste text into the text area.
  • Import File: Upload a TXT or CSV file to load text.
  • Format Text: Select text and click Bold, Italic, or Underline buttons to apply formatting.
  • Enable Auto-Save: Check "Auto-Save to Browser" to save notes in local storage.
  • Track Counts: View word and character counts in real-time.
  • Copy: Click "Copy Notes" to copy to your clipboard.
  • Download: Click "Download (TXT)" or "Download (CSV)" to save notes as a file.
  • Reset: Click "Reset" to clear the text area and local storage.

The tool works locally in your browser and supports offline use after loading.

Use Cases for Online Notepad

This tool is versatile for various scenarios:

  • Students: Take lecture notes or draft essays.
  • Professionals: Record meeting minutes or project ideas.
  • Writers: Draft articles or stories with basic formatting.
  • Developers: Store code snippets or documentation notes.
