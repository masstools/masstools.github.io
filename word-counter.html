About the Word Counter

The Word Counter by Mass Tools is a free tool to analyze text. Count words, characters, sentences, and paragraphs instantly. All processing is done in your browser for privacy.

Why Use the Word Counter?

Text analysis is useful for:

  • Writing: Ensure essays or articles meet word count requirements.
  • SEO: Optimize content length for search engines.
  • Editing: Check text structure for readability.
  • Social Media: Fit posts within character limits.

How to Use the Word Counter

Follow these steps:

  • Paste Text: Type or paste text into the textarea.
  • Count: Click "Count" to see results.
  • Clear: Click "Clear" to reset the textarea and results.
