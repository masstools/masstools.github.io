About the QR Code Generator Tool

The QR Code Generator Tool by Mass Tools is a free tool to create QR codes from text or URLs. Generate and download QR codes securely in your browser.

Why Use the QR Code Generator Tool?

This tool is perfect for creating scannable QR codes. Use it to:

  • Share Links: Create QR codes for websites or social media profiles.
  • Business Promotion: Add QR codes to business cards or flyers.
  • Event Management: Generate QR codes for event tickets or check-ins.
  • Education: Share resources or assignments via QR codes.

How to Use the QR Code Generator Tool

Our QR Code Generator Tool is easy to use. Follow these steps:

  • Enter Text: Input a URL, text, or any data in the input field.
  • Generate: Click "Generate QR Code" to create the QR code.
  • Preview: View the QR code in the display area.
  • Download: Click "Download QR Code" to save as a PNG file.
  • Reset: Click "Reset" to clear the input and QR code.

The tool processes data locally and works offline after loading.
