About the Text to ASCII Converter

The Text to ASCII Converter by Mass Tools is a free tool to convert text to ASCII codes. Paste text or upload TXT/DOCX files for instant results in decimal or JSON format, processed in your browser for privacy.

Why Use the Text to ASCII Converter?

Converting text to ASCII is useful for encoding and programming. Use it to:

  • Programming: Generate ASCII codes for string manipulation.
  • Data Encoding: Convert text for use in protocols or systems.
  • Debugging: Analyze character codes in text data.
  • Education: Learn about ASCII encoding and character sets.

How to Use the Text to ASCII Converter

Our Text to ASCII Converter is simple and intuitive. Follow these steps:

  • Paste Text: Type or paste text into the input textarea.
  • Upload File: Drag and drop or click to upload a TXT or DOCX file.
  • Choose Format: Select decimal (space-separated) or JSON output.
  • Convert: Click "Convert to ASCII" to see the result.
  • Copy or Download: Copy the result to clipboard or download as a file.
  • Reset: Click "Reset" to clear inputs and results.

The tool supports drag-and-drop and works offline after loading. Files are processed locally.
