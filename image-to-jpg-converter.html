Why Convert Images to JPG?

JPG (or JPEG) is one of the most popular image formats due to its balance of quality and file size. According to Wikipedia, JPG uses lossy compression, which reduces file size while maintaining acceptable visual quality, making it ideal for web use and sharing. Here are some key benefits of using JPG:

  • Smaller File Sizes: JPG files are typically smaller than PNG or WebP, saving storage space and reducing website load times.
  • Wide Compatibility: JPG is supported by virtually all devices, browsers, and platforms, unlike newer formats like WebP.
  • Adjustable Quality: Our tool lets you control the compression level (from 0.1 to 1.0), allowing you to prioritize quality or file size.
