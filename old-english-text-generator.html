About the Old English Text Generator Tool

The Old English Text Generator tool by Mass Tools converts text to Old English (Blackletter/Gothic) style using Unicode characters. Paste text or upload TXT, CSV, or XLSX files for instant processing.

Why Use This Tool?

  • Historical Aesthetic: Create text with a medieval or Gothic look for designs, tattoos, or social media.
  • Versatility: Choose between serif (Fraktur) and sans-serif (Gothic) styles for different visual effects.
  • Compatibility: Unicode characters work in plain text environments like Discord or X.
  • Batch Processing: Handle large text datasets efficiently.

How to Use

  • Select an Old English style (Sans-Serif or Serif).
  • Paste text (e.g., "Old English text") or upload a TXT, CSV, or XLSX file.
  • Click "Generate Old English Text" to view results (e.g., Sans-Serif: "ùï∫ùï∑ùïØ ùï∞ùïπùï≤ùï∑ùï¥ùïæùï≥ ùñôùñäùñùùñô").
  • Use "Undo" or "Redo" to revert or restore changes.
  • Copy or download the processed text.
  • Reset to clear inputs and results.

Tips

  • Use files under 10MB for best performance.
  • Sans-Serif (Gothic) is cleaner and modern; Serif (Fraktur) is more ornate.
  • Combine with Bold Text Generator or Italic Text Generator for enhanced styling.
  • Not all characters have Old English equivalents; unsupported characters remain unchanged.
