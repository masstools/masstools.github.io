About the Email Extractor

The Email Extractor by Mass Tools is a free tool to extract email addresses from text, URLs, or files. Ideal for marketing, lead generation, or data processing, it processes data locally in your browser for privacy. Important: Ensure compliance with privacy laws like GDPR or CAN-SPAM Act when using extracted emails.

Why Use the Email Extractor?

This tool streamlines collecting email addresses for various purposes:

  • Marketing Campaigns: Build targeted email lists from public sources.
  • Lead Generation: Extract emails from websites or documents for sales outreach.
  • Data Processing: Organize contact lists from mixed data sources.
  • Research: Gather emails for surveys or outreach with permission.

How to Use the Email Extractor

Follow these steps to extract emails:

  • Paste Text: Copy and paste text containing emails into the text area.
  • Enter URL: Provide a website URL to scan for emails (ensure permission).
  • Upload File: Upload a TXT, PDF, or CSV file to extract emails.
  • Set Options: Enable "Unique Emails" to remove duplicates, "Sort Alphabetically" for ordered output, or add a domain filter (e.g., example.com).
  • Extract: Click "Extract" to find email addresses.
  • Copy: Click "Copy Emails" to copy to your clipboard.
  • Download: Click "Download (CSV)" to save as a CSV file.
  • Reset: Click "Reset" to clear inputs and settings.

The tool works locally in your browser and supports offline use after loading.
