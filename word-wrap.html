About the Word Wrap Converter Tool

The Word Wrap Converter tool by Mass Tools wraps text to a specified line length, breaking at word boundaries where possible. Paste text or upload TXT, CSV, or XLSX files for instant processing.

Why Use This Tool?

  • Text Formatting: Format text for emails, code comments, or terminal displays.
  • Readability: Break long lines for better readability in documents or interfaces.
  • Compatibility: Ensure text fits within fixed-width constraints for legacy systems.
  • Batch Processing: Handle large text datasets efficiently.

How to Use

  • Enter the desired wrap length (e.g., 80).
  • Paste text (e.g., "This is a long sentence that needs wrapping") or upload a TXT, CSV, or XLSX file.
  • Check "Trim Output" to remove leading and trailing spaces.
  • Click "Wrap Text" to view results (e.g., lines wrapped at 80 characters).
  • Use "Undo" or "Redo" to revert or restore changes.
  • Copy or download the processed text.
  • Reset to clear inputs and results.

Tips

  • Use files under 10MB for best performance.
  • Combine with Pad Text Converter or Remove Extra Spaces for advanced text processing.
