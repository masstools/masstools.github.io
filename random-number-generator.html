How to Use the Random Number Generator

Our Random Number Generator is simple to use. Follow these steps:

  • Set Count: Choose the number of numbers (1-100).
  • Define Range: Set minimum and maximum values (-1000 to 1000).
  • Specify Format: Enter decimal places (0 for integers, up to 5 for decimals).
  • Choose Separator: Select comma, new line, or space.
  • Enable Unique: Check "Unique Numbers" to avoid duplicates.
  • Generate: Click "Generate" to create random numbers.
  • Copy: Click "Copy Numbers" to copy to your clipboard.
  • Reset: Click "Reset" to restore default settings.

The tool works locally in your browser for privacy and supports offline use after loading.
