About the Image to HEIC Converter

The Image to HEIC Converter by Mass Tools is a free tool that converts raster images (JPG, PNG, GIF, BMP, etc.) into HEIC format. HEIC (High Efficiency Image Coding) is a modern format developed by the MPEG group, offering superior compression and quality compared to JPEG. This tool processes conversions in your browser, ensuring privacy and speed without server uploads.

Why Convert Images to HEIC?

HEIC is widely used on Apple devices (iOS, macOS) and increasingly supported on Android and Windows. According to Wikipedia, HEIC uses HEVC (H.265) compression for smaller file sizes with better quality. Key benefits include:

  • High Quality: HEIC retains excellent image quality at smaller file sizes than JPEG.
  • Efficient Compression: Up to 50% smaller than JPEG, ideal for storage and sharing.
  • Modern Features: Supports transparency, live photos, bursts, and 10-bit color.
  • Device Compatibility: Native on iOS/macOS; growing support on Android/Windows.
