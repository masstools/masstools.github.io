About the Image to EXR Converter

The Image to EXR Converter by Mass Tools is a free tool that converts raster images (JPG, PNG, GIF, etc.) into EXR (OpenEXR) files. EXR is a high-dynamic-range (HDR) format used in VFX, animation, and rendering. This tool processes images in your browser, ensuring privacy and speed without server uploads. Note: This generates a single-layer RGB EXR; for advanced features, use software like Photoshop or Nuke.

Why Convert Images to EXR?

EXR, developed by Industrial Light & Magic, supports high bit-depth and multiple channels. According to Wikipedia, EXR is ideal for professional imaging. Key benefits include:

  • HDR Support: Store high-dynamic-range pixel data for VFX.
  • High Precision: Support 16-bit or 32-bit float for accurate colors.
  • Compatibility: Used in tools like Maya, Blender, and Nuke.
  • Flexibility: Suitable for compositing and rendering pipelines.

How to Use the Image to EXR Converter

Our Image to EXR Converter is simple and supports customization. Follow these steps:

  • Upload Your Image: Click the upload area or drag and drop your image (JPG, PNG, GIF, etc.).
  • Select Bit Depth: Choose 16-bit Float (smaller file) or 32-bit Float (higher precision).
  • Convert: Click "Convert to EXR" to process the image. The original image is previewed.
  • Download: Click "Download EXR" to save the file, named with "masstools.github.io" (e.g., converted-image-masstools.github.io.exr).
  • Reset: Click "Reset" to clear uploads and start over.

The tool supports drag-and-drop and works offline after loading. Note: EXR files can be large; ensure your software supports OpenEXR.

To get the best results from EXR conversion, consider these tips:
