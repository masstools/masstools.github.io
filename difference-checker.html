About the Difference Checker Tool

The Difference Checker by Mass Tools compares two texts and highlights differences. Supports file uploads and customizable comparison options.

Why Use This Tool?

  • Editing: Track changes in documents or code.
  • Data Analysis: Compare datasets or logs.
  • Collaboration: Review revisions between versions.
  • Debugging: Identify differences in outputs.

How to Use

  • Paste two texts or upload TXT, CSV, or XLSX files.
  • Choose options: case sensitive or ignore whitespace.
  • Click "Compare Texts" to view differences.
  • Copy or download the comparison results.
  • Reset to clear inputs and results.

Tips

  • Use files under 10MB for best performance.
  • For CSV/XLSX, ensure single-column data for accurate comparison.
  • Combine with Find and Replace for editing.
