How to Use the Remove Unwanted Characters Tool

Our Remove Unwanted Characters Tool is simple to use. Follow these steps:

  • Specify Characters: Enter characters (e.g., @#$%) or select predefined options (punctuation, special characters, numbers).
  • Paste Text: Enter text (e.g., hello@world!#$%).
  • Upload File (Optional): Drag and drop or click to upload a TXT, CSV, or DOCX file.
  • Process: Click "Remove Characters" to see the result with specified characters removed.
  • Copy or Download: Copy the processed text to clipboard or download as a TXT file.
  • Reset: Click "Reset" to clear inputs and results.

The tool supports drag-and-drop and works offline after loading. Files are processed locally.
