Why Convert Images to PNG?

PNG (Portable Network Graphics) is a popular format known for its lossless compression and support for transparency. According to Wikipedia, PNG is widely used for images requiring high quality or transparent backgrounds. Here are some key benefits of using PNG:

  • Lossless Compression: PNG preserves image quality without loss, unlike JPG’s lossy compression.
  • Transparency Support: PNG supports alpha channels for transparent backgrounds, perfect for logos and graphics.
  • Wide Compatibility: PNG is supported by most browsers, design software, and platforms.
