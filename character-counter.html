About the Character Counter

The Character Counter by Mass Tools is a free tool to analyze text by counting characters (with/without spaces), words, and sentences. Paste text or upload TXT/DOCX files, processed securely in your browser.

Why Use the Character Counter?

This tool is perfect for writers, developers, and students. Use it to:

  • Social Media: Check tweet or post length.
  • SEO: Optimize meta descriptions or content length.
  • Writing: Meet word/character limits for essays or articles.
  • Development: Validate input lengths for forms.

How to Use the Character Counter

Our Character Counter is simple to use. Follow these steps:

  • Paste Text: Enter text in the textarea.
  • Upload File (Optional): Drag and drop or click to upload a TXT or DOCX file.
  • Count: Click "Count Text" to see results.
  • Copy or Download: Copy results to clipboard or download as a TXT file.
  • Reset: Click "Reset" to clear inputs and results.

The tool supports drag-and-drop and works offline after loading. Files are processed locally.

Use Cases for Character Counter

The Character Counter is ideal for various scenarios, including:

  • Content Creation: Ensure blog posts meet length requirements.
  • Academic Writing: Verify essay word counts.
  • Programming: Check string lengths for code.
  • Marketing: Craft concise ad copy.

Tips for Using the Character Counter

To get the best results, consider these tips:
