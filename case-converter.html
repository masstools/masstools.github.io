About the Case Converter

The Case Converter by Mass Tools is a free tool to transform text case. Process text in your browser for privacy.

Why Use the Case Converter?

Text case conversion is useful for:

  • Formatting: Standardize text for documents or websites.
  • Editing: Adjust case for consistency in writing.
  • Coding: Format variable names or strings.
  • Content Creation: Prepare text for social media or blogs.

How to Use the Case Converter

Follow these steps:

  • Paste Text: Type or paste text into the textarea.
  • Choose Case: Click Uppercase, Lowercase, or Title Case.
  • View Results: Text updates instantly.
  • Reset: Click "Reset" to clear text.
